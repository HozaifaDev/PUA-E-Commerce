Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Orders" section. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery for local orders.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for 1-2 day delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for most items. Please ensure items are in their original condition with all packaging intact.
